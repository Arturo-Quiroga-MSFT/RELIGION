# World Religions Research

A thorough, evidence-based research project examining world religions through
the lens of historical scholarship, archaeology, and textual criticism.

## Guiding Principles

- **Scholarly rigor**: rely on peer-reviewed scholarship, primary sources, and
  archaeological evidence.
- **No theological bias**: treat every tradition with the same critical and
  respectful methodology.
- **Fact vs. fiction**: clearly distinguish historically attested events from
  faith claims, legends, and later traditions.
- **Transparent sourcing**: cite sources for every major claim so readers can
  verify independently.

## Research Order

1. **Christianity** *(in progress)*
2. Islam
3. Judaism
4. Hinduism
5. Buddhism
6. Other traditions (Sikhism, Jainism, Zoroastrianism, indigenous religions …)
